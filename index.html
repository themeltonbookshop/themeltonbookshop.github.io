The Melton Bookshop

Independent Booksellers in Melton Mowbray, Leicestershire

To all our Customers and Friends,

I am afraid we have some news to share that (whilst many of you may have suspected) many will find a shock.

We have made the decision not to carry on the lease for the premises when it runs out 25th September. We have also decided to close the business itself.

As you can imagine this is a hard choice for us to make but the financial pressures of running a business and the low foot-fall on Leicester St. mean that we don't feel we can carry on as it currently stands.

We want to thank everyone that has been a loyal customer of the shop and supported us throughout the last 3 years and indeed since 1975!

We plan to continue trading until the end of August and more details will be released as time progresses.

As always, I hope this finds you well.

Catherine x
